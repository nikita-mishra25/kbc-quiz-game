1. If the answer is correct then the player wins the corresponding amount of prize and proceeds to the next question.
  2. If the answer is incorrect then the game ends. and the player takes the corresponding amount of the prize.
  3. The game ends either when the player gives an incorrect answer or successfully answers all 10 questions.
  4. The player's total winnings are displayed at the end of the game.
  5. The player has the option to use lifelines during the game.
  6. Three lifelines are available: 50:50, Phone a Friend, and Audience Poll
  7. The player can use lifelines at any question, but only three lifelines are allowed in the entire game.
  8. 50:50 Lifeline: Removes two incorrect options and display remaining two option and one wrong option.
  9. Phone a Friend: Pool for the answer from the friend.
  10. Audience Poll: Pool for the answer from the audience.
